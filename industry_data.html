Industry Data

Job Title/Rankings

The job titles associated with our groups’ ideal jobs are-

  • IT Technician (support, service desk, etc)
  • Java Developer
  • Web Developer
  • Software Developer
  • Programmer/Developer (application, software, analyst, database etc)
  • Front-end Developer

    • All of these jobs are in high demand with a predicted job growth rate of 23.4% over the next few years (2019-2024) according to Seek. However, the job growth rate for a Web Developer is 9.2% (2019-2024) which is lower than all of the other jobs. As nearly all of the jobs have the same predicted growth rate according to Seek, we can use the job opportunities to rank them.

    Ranking in terms of job openings at the time of researching:

    1) Developer- 5,931
    2) Web Developer- 2,508
    3) Programmer- 1,865
    4) Java Developer- 1,394
    5) Front-end Developer- 876
    6) IT Technician- 365

    The IT Technician job role has been predicted to increase by 23.4% with the least amount of job openings indicating that this position is filled quickly and that there are plenty of professionals who meet the requirements for these roles. The Developer job title had the most job openings which portrays that employers are looking for more and more professionals with the necessary skills to acquire the positions.

    These general skills in our skill set are highly sought after by employers with time management being the most valued followed by teamwork/collaboration, problem solving skill, emotional intelligence, creativity, adaptability, professionalism and more.
